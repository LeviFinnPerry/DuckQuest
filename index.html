Kudos:

Recognition and praise for heroic deeds accomplished. The higher your Kudos score, the greater your renown and prestige

1

You have 10 points to distribute between your heart and Psyche

Each must contain at least one point. Choose wisely, or don't!

Heart:

Heart is a measure of an Adventurer's physical health and perseverance in the face of adversity. Or more simply, how many “hits to kill” your duck.

Psyche:

This is the mental vitality of your Adventurer. The duck brain can only suffer so much strain before quacking up.

Armour:

Anything that makes your duck harder to hurt is represented by your Armour score. You also get a point of Armour for each level of Kudos your Adventurer has.

1

QUACKtributes

Each Quacktribute needs a number 1-5 assigned to it, the trick? You can only use each number once!

Hint: 1 is your ducks weak point, 5 is your ducks greatest talent!

Quickness:

Refers to your speed and coordination. Are you keenly aware of your surroundings and fleet of foot or a clumsy klutz?

Ugly:

Ugly helps you resist poison, harsh environmental conditions, and dangerous falls. It’s also your ability to lift heavy things, bust open doors, crush and wreck stuff.

Arcana:

This is the brainpower stat; your broad intellect, and aptitude for magick. Your mystic acumen and mental adroitness.

Cool:

A measure of your force of personality; your presence, bearing, and social influence. This may involve some notoriety and status but also includes your empathy, intuition and willpower.

Kismet:

Kismet works in mysterious ways. Some call it destiny, fate or blind luck. Kismet can be used for affecting your Adventurer or their environment.
